Classy Housing Perunnas is a premium real estate developer that creates luxury residential properties with modern design, strategic locations, and guaranteed security. We focus on providing comfortable and elegant homes that enhance your lifestyle.

Our properties are located in prime neighborhoods across major metropolitan areas, providing easy access to schools, shopping centers, entertainment venues, and business districts. Each location is carefully selected to ensure convenience and a high quality of life.

Our housing prices vary based on location, size, and amenities. We offer a range of properties from luxury apartments to premium villas, with prices starting from $450,000. Each property is designed with high-end finishes and modern amenities to provide exceptional value.
